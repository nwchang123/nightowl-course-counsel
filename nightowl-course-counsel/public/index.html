About NightOwl Course Counsel

NightOwl Course Counsel is a course chatbot powered by the AI of ChatGPT 3.5 Turbo. It serves as a comprehensive resource for students seeking information about various courses. Our primary goal is to assist students in quickly finding their ideal courses.


Our Mission

At NightOwl, our mission is to empower students to make informed decisions about their education. We believe that every student deserves access to quality guidance and support as they embark on their educational journey.


Our Team

Night Owl is the name of our team, consisting of three dedicated developers. This website is the result of an assignment undertaken by Computer Science students at the University of Technology Sarawak.


Our Commitment

We are committed to providing our services free of charge. You don't have to pay anything to benefit from our website.
